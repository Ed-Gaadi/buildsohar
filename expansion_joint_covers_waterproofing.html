Expansion Joint Covers & Waterproofing

Expansion joints are used to separate slabs and concrete from other parts of the structure. Expansion joints allow independent movement between adjoining structural members, minimizing cracking when such movements are restrained. Buildings are often constructed using flexible materials on the roof and structural expansion joints. We treat gaps depending on sizes and provide solutions to make it watertight with suitable covers.
